Mental model — how to describe your day

Think of each event as what + when. Chain multiple events with a comma or "and". Dayli handles the rest.

[event name] at [time]
[event name] from [start] to [end]
[event name] at [time] for [duration]

Time formats

  • at 9am or at 9:30am
  • from 9 to 5 or 9–5
  • for 30 min / 1 hour
  • tomorrow at 7 — assumes am for early hours
  • Monday, next Friday

Multiple events

  • Separate with a comma: gym at 7, work 9–5
  • Use "and": lunch at 1 and call at 3
  • List your whole day in one sentence

Editing existing events

  • move gym to tomorrow
  • reschedule my 3pm call to 4pm
  • make my meeting shorter

Flexible scheduling

  • make my afternoon less busy
  • free up my morning
  • lighter day tomorrow

Common event defaults

  • gym → 7–8am if no time given
  • work → 9am–5pm
  • dinner → 7–8pm
  • lunch → 12–1pm

Full-day example

  • gym at 7, work 9–12, lunch at 1, team standup at 2 for 30 min, dinner at 7
